Privacy Policy

The False News Detector Chrome Extension analyzes visible text on web pages to detect potentially false or misleading statements using a secure AI proxy. No personally identifiable data is collected, stored, or shared.

The extension does not log any browsing history, identity, or content beyond what is temporarily analyzed. No data is sold or used for third-party purposes.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the developer at https://github.com/AjayPlus.
